About

A startup company based in Chemnitz, formed by a group of people having a same aim to build strong connections between people and mass media with the help of cutting edge technology.


To become world's leading company that innovates and strengthens connections between people and mass media with cutting-edge technologies.

Our mission is to achieve strong connection between people and mass media by unlocking the potential of human creativity on technologies.

Our purpose is to improve relationship between human and mass media by bringing up the new methods and approaches.
